                              what is reasoning?

<paragraph><sentence>in  the  history of research on thinking two (or more) distinct traditions have
persisted.</sentence>  <sentence>one tradition, deriving from the study of language and logic, views
thinking  as  a form of logical and verbal reasoning.</sentence>  <sentence>the other, deriving from
the study of human problem solving, views thinking as a  process  of  heuristic
search  through  a mental representation of a problem situation.</sentence>  <sentence>the talk will
use recent findings from research on  human  thinking  to  review  the  present
status and future prospects within a.i.</sentence> <sentence>of these two paradigms.</sentence></paragraph>
