 title:		space robotics activities at the johnson space center

<paragraph>space missions and unique considerations for space environments 
are described. the shuttle remote manipulator system is described in 
detail, and representative space missions are discussed. the space station 
freedom robots are described and their long term operations are discussed. 
the johnson space center development of the eva (extravehicular activity)
helper - retriever robot is discussed. advanced technologies under 
development for future robotic applications are discussed including dexterous 
hands, high performance manipulators, fault tolerant robotic joints, and 
vision-based intelligent robots.</paragraph>
